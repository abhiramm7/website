Convolution

Convolution is a mathematical operation akin to addition or multiplication. It operates on two signals1 to generate a combined signal.

\[ (f * g)(t):=\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} f(\tau) g(t-\tau) d \tau \]

  1. Signals in this context can be continuous or discrete.↩︎
